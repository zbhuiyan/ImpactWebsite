Consent Narrative

Hello,

We (Zarin, Sean, David, Becca, and Celina) are students at Olin College of Engineering taking a user experience design class. Our goal is to design an interface or application to help with an important and relevant problem which affects a group of people, potentially including you. We are hoping you will allow us to ask you questions that help us in our project. We will be building a working prototype to illustrate proof of concept. It is unlikely that we will create a finished product.

We expect this to take approximately 30-45 minutes. We hope that our interaction is as much of a learning experience for you as it is for us. Please let us know if you are comfortable talking with us. Your privacy will be fully respected and we will not share any personally identifiable information. You are welcome to state ‘I don’t want to talk about this’ at any time, and we will respect this. We want this to be pleasant for everyone!

There is a possibility that we may want to document our interaction and we will explicitly ask for your permission before doing so. If you would like to change your mind at any time, feel free to let us know.
